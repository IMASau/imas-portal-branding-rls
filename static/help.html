Reef Life Survey Data Portal Help

The IMOS (Integrated Marine Observing System) Data Portal User Guide provides a set of instructions for navigating the portal features. The screenshots in this guide are taken from the IMOS portal, but the instructions are also applicable to the Reef Life Survey Data Portal and provide a useful framework for discovering data.

For specific questions regarding the Reef Life Survey Data Portal, or for further information, contact the IMAS Data Manager: IMAS.DataManager@utas.edu.au

Links to further IMAS data resources can be accessed from the IMAS research data home page.
